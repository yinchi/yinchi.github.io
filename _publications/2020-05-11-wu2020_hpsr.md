### Brief:

We propose a framework to analyze performance metrics for 5G millimeter wave (mmWave) networks, based on stochastic geometry, teletraffic models, and the classical Erlang Fixed Point Approximation method. Our framework takes into account practical concerns in mmWave networks including physical blockages, capacity constraints, and the stochastic nature of mobile traffic. Numerical results demonstrate our method is accurate and computationally efficient.
