### Brief:

We use concepts and insights from traditional teletraffic theory, including resource sharing, trunk reservation, and mutual overflow, to design a new patient referral policy to improve ICU network efficiency. We also develop an approximation for the evaluating the network performance under the policy and demonstrate its accuracy and computational efficiency.
