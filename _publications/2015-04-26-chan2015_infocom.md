### Brief:

We consider an overflow loss system consisting of a set of finite-buffer processor-sharing (PS) queues, and develop effective methods for evaluation of its blocking probability based on decomposition of the system into independent PS queues. Unlike existing decomposition approximations, we apply decomposition to a <i>surrogate model</i> of the original system, with more accurate results.
