### Brief:

We consider a cellular mobile network, modeling each BS with sleeping mechanism as an M/G/1/K queue with vacations, and the entire cellular network as a network of such queues. A robust, scalable and computationally efficient analytical method is proposed to evaluate grade-of-service metrics (including mean delay and blocking probability) and power consumption under each scheme.
